## 2. Key Features Observed

### 🟡 India – Delhi Metro Rail


Features noticed:
- Journey planner for checking metro routes between stations.
- Detailed metro route map available for passengers.
- Fare calculator to estimate ticket prices.
- Information about station facilities and metro schedules(First/Last Train time).
- Latest news and announcements related to metro services.
- Notices and alerts section displaying important announcements and service updates.(maintenance information,official notices)
- Mobile application available for travel planning.(DMRC app)
- AI assistant chatbot available on the website to help users.

My observation:  
- The route map and fare calculator help passengers easily plan their journeys before travelling.
- The website layout is simple and provides important travel information clearly.
- The AI assistant is very useful because passengers can quickly ask questions and get information about routes, tickets, or services. A similar chatbot could be implemented in a Sri Lanka metro website to help users easily find travel information.
- A similar system would be very useful for a future Sri Lanka metro network.

---

### 🔵 USA – Los Angeles Metro


Features noticed:
- Trip planner for finding the fastest route between locations.
- Detailed information about bus and train routes.
- Real-time service alerts for delays and disruptions.
- Ticket and fare information for different types of passengers.
- Reduced fare and discount programs are available for different passenger groups such as students, employees like.
- Accessibility options for disabled passengers.
- Mobile app available for travel planning and updates.(DRIR_Appendix_F_MOBILE)
- Safety and security information section available on the website to help passengers travel safely.
- The website allows users to report incidents through the Transit Watch app or by sending a text message.
- Emergency contact options such as calling 911 are clearly displayed for quick access.
- Language selection feature that allows users to view the website in different languages.

My observation:  
- The real-time alerts and trip planning tools are very helpful for passengers. 
- These features allow travellers to stay updated about service changes and plan their journeys more efficiently.
- The safety section is very important because it helps passengers feel secure while travelling. Features such as incident reporting, emergency contact options, and security cameras improve passenger safety.
- A similar system could be implemented in a Sri Lanka metro website to ensure safe travel for passengers.



---

### 🔴 United Kingdom – Transport for London


Features noticed:
- Interactive journey planner for metro, buses and other transport.
- Detailed underground route maps for London transport.
- Live service status updates for each metro line.
- Information about ticket prices and payment methods.
- Travel information for tourists and visitors.
- Mobile friendly design with a dedicated transport mobile app.(TFL GO)
- Users can enter their starting point and destination to find the best route.
- Passengers can also find information about discounts, cheaper travel options, and refund or replacement services for travel cards.
- Additional information about suppliers, contractors, and the history of London buses is also available on the website.


My observation:  
- The Transport for London website provides clear travel information and real-time service status updates. 
- The journey planner and route maps make it easy for passengers to understand the metro system. 
- Features like contactless payment and mobile apps make the travel experience faster and more convenient. 
- This type of information could also be useful for a Sri Lanka metro website to show system performance, service management, and operational updates.

## 5. My Personal Opinion

After reviewing these metro websites, I noticed that journey planning and clear route maps are the most useful features for passengers. The London Transport website provides excellent real-time updates and clear maps, while the Delhi Metro website offers useful travel and fare information. The USA metro website provides helpful safety features such as incident reporting, emergency contact options, and support through mobile apps. Language selection also makes the website easier to use for passengers from different backgrounds. 
If a Sri Lanka metro system is developed in the future, the most important features should be a journey planner, route maps and accurate fare information. These features would help passengers easily understand the metro network and plan their travel.
