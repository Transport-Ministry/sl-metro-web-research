## 2. Key Features Observed

### 🔵 Hong Kong – MTR Corporation
![Hong KOng MTR Corporation Homepage](.home/wishvadini/sl-metro-web-research/screenshots/MTR.png)
*Screenshot taken: 2025-03-09*

**Features noticed:**
- Octopus card integration for payments and top-ups
- Real-time service updates with live maps and timers
- Journey planner including buses and ferries
- Sustainability and green initiatives highlighted
- Mobile app with AR station guides

**My observation:** Advanced payment system
Could inspire Sri Lanka to use e-wallet payments to reduce cash queues

---

### 🔴 New York – MTA
![MTA Homepage](/home/wishvadini/sl-metro-web-research/screenshots/MTA.png)
*Screenshot taken: 2025-03-09*

**Features noticed:**
- Live arrival predictions for subway lines
- Interactive system map with alerts and service changes
- Fare machine locator and contactless payment guides
- Accessibility tools (step-free station finder)
- Open data portal for developers

**My observation:** Strong real-time information system ; Useful for managing traffic and disruptions in Sri Lanka

---

### 🟠 Paris – RATP
![Paris – RATP Homepage](/home/wishvadini/sl-metro-web-research/screenshots/Tokyo.png)
*Screenshot taken: 2025-03-09*

**Features noticed:**
- Interactive metro map with zoom and transfer points
- Multilingual journey planner
- Station amenities search (Wi-Fi, elevators, bike parking)
- Eco-friendly travel information
- Lost property tracking system

**My observation:** Focus on tourism and station details ; Good model for visitors travelling in Colombo

## 5. My Personal Opinion

> *Write this in your own words. What did you personally find most useful? What do you think Sri Lanka needs most?*

I personally think the Hong Kong MTR website is the most user-friendly one out there.It makes trip planning so simple and efficient with its fare tools and real-time updates—perfect even for newcomers.

Sri Lanka should definitely focus on mobile-first design and multilingual support for Sinhala, Tamil, and English.Keeping things simple and easy to use matters way more than overloading it with features.
