## 5. My Personal Opinion

Singapore's TransitLink impressed me most—its clean fare calculator and mobile focus feel intuitive, even on slow connections, which matches Sri Lanka's 4G reality. No overload of features; just what riders need first.

Sri Lanka needs multilingual support and real-time status above all. With diverse users and traffic woes, clear Sinhala/Tamil updates could build trust fast. Prioritize mobile-first simplicity over flashy designs—emulate Singapore, adapt Delhi's languages.
